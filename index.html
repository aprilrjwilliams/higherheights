Mission Statement

We believe that Children have the right to a nurturing, safe, Christian environment where they feel comfortable and secure. We believe that children have the right to develop to their maximum abilities and that the teachers have the responsibility to provide opportunities for this development to occur. We recognize that each child is an individual and that each Child must be allowed to develop at his or her own pace. We hope that in treating each child with love and respect that they in return will learn to treat others with the same love and respect. We will strive to thoughtfully plan activities that meet the needs of every child which follow early Christian childhood developmental principals.

Curriculum

The objectives Of our curriculum are based on proven child developmental principles regarding how children best grow and learn. We will provide children with a responsive learning environment with various experiences to help them develop socially, intellectually, physically, and emotionally in a manner appropriate to their age and stage of development.

Programs

6 weeks - 1 year

In the infant program you will see infant teachers talk and sing with your child during feeding, diapering and playtime. This interaction helps your baby begin to understand language and conversation. Staff pays close attention to all aspects of your child’s development

2 years - 3 years

In the early preschool program, teachers actively partner with parents to help your child learn important independent life skills like potty training and hand washing. Staff pays close attention to all aspects of your child’s development.

3 years - School age

Teachers guide and extend children’s thinking through purposeful play, meaningful questioning and direct instruction. Your child is engaged and having fun, not even aware that he/she’s developing skills to prepare her for reading and math.
